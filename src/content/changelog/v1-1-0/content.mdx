### Added

- Billing behind a provider adapter, with hosted checkout and a customer portal.
  Subscription state in the database is derived from webhooks, never guessed from
  a redirect the user might never follow.
- Signature-verified, idempotent webhook handling: a redelivered event cannot
  double-charge or duplicate a row, and an out-of-order retry cannot overwrite
  newer state.
- A super-admin panel with global user and organization views, account
  suspension, and soft deletion with a retention window.
- Impersonation for support, disclosed by a banner on every page and recorded in
  an audit log.

### Fixed

- The last Owner of an organization can no longer be demoted or removed.
