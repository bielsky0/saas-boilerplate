With the app running, this walks you through the flows the boilerplate ships.

## Create an account

Go to `/signup` and register. Because email is set to log mode by default, the
verification link appears in your terminal instead of an inbox. Click it, and you
land on the dashboard with a verified account and a personal account already
created for you.

## Start an organization

A personal account is a tenant of one. To collaborate, create an organization
from the dashboard: pick a name, and a URL slug is derived from it.

You become the Owner. An organization always keeps at least one Owner — the app
refuses to remove or demote the last one, because an organization nobody can
administer is unrecoverable.

## Invite a teammate

From the members page, invite an email address and choose a role:

- **Owner** — full control, including billing and deletion.
- **Admin** — manages members and settings, but not billing.
- **Member** — uses the product without administering it.

The invitation link is single-use and expires. It works whether or not the
recipient already has an account: if they do not, they register first and join
automatically afterwards.

## Switch context

The switcher in the navigation moves you between your personal account and each
organization. The active tenant is always visible in the URL, so a link to a
resource is a link to that resource in that organization — refreshing or sharing
the page keeps the context.
